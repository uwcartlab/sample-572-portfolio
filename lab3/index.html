This explainer describes a web map based on the abstract, modernist art of Piet Mondrian.

The map makes use of the large blocks of yellow, red, and blue, and the thick, black lines that Mondrian was known for.

Chicago, Illinois looks like this.

Yellowstone National Park, and similar natural areas, appear wide open, reflecting the lack of human-made geometric lines and angles Mondrian was known for.

The San Jaun islands emphasize the deep blue blocks of water.

Finally, a look at the Wausau area in Northern Wisconsin shows the blend of color blocks and red geometric lines charateristic of Mondrian.
